About Corbin

More Than a Loan Officer. A Trusted Advisor.

Corbin Claypool is a Fresno-based loan officer at Vero Mortgage with a mission that goes beyond closing deals — he's on a mission to give every homebuyer and homeowner access to the education they need to make confident, wealth-building decisions.

When Corbin saw a gap in real estate education in the Central Valley, he built Everything About Mortgage to fill it. Whether it's jiu jitsu, family, or real estate investing — he shows up with the same intensity for everything he does.

How It Works
